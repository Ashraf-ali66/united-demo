Educational Tour

on Jan 1,2022 All the students of our UEBS are informed that School Tour Committee has decided to organise an educational tour to Lumbini,Palpa and Pokhara from 10th May to 15th May. The students of class-X were allowed to take part in the tour. The fee for the tour is 3000/- per head. The students were assembled in school ground on 10th May in the morning.The primary purpose of an educational tour or a field tour is to educate students, and provide them with a chance to learn from a new set of experiences in an informal setting. In addition to this, a school tour also allows students to develop close bonds with teachers and classmates. Here are some benefits of educational tours.

Benefits of Educational Tours

  • A field tour acts as the foundation for improved learning experiences. They can be an excellent break for both students and teachers. As mentioned earlier, educational tours not only educate, but prove to be a good medium for developing stronger relationships.

  • With proper guidance and support from teachers, educational tours turn out to be mobile classrooms. Students are able to learn from fun and interactive sessions outside the monotonous school routine.
  • On school trips, students are able to socialize and interact with people from all walks of life. Whether you choose a new city or a day excursion, these trips are a chance to learn from fresh perspectives.
