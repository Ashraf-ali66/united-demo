Manoj Moktan -Vice-Principal



" A good educational institution is one that helps the overall development of a child's body, mind and character. Therefore, at UEBS, we value each student as an individual and as a part of the community. We develop all aspects of their personhood with excellence. To achieve this goal we provide a wide array of programmes and activities so that student can seize the opportunities afforded to him. A place where each and every aspect of a child's personality is honed like basic intelligence, motor skills, thinking, problem solving observation power and so on. Institutions pave a way for a child's overall development, and so shall we take it as my responsibility to ensure that every student who enters this school leaves as a responsible and celebrated citizen of the country and makes everyone proud.
