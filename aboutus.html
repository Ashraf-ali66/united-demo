ABOUT US

 Posted on December 4, 2022 by Ashraf Ali

United English Boarding School established in 2057 B.S with the prime objective of providing quality education in English medium is located in Manahari Rural Municipality-8,Makawanpur Bagmati Province.

The school enrolls students from pre-primary to tenth grade. Pre-primary grades the most formative years in the child life and school takes pride in offering a mastery approach to learning. The school has a unique learning environment where dedicated teachers inspire students to realize their full potential to be better members of global community.

Our vision is to develop well rounded, confident and respon- sible individuals who aspire to achieve their full potential. We will do this by providing welcoming, happy, safe and supportive learning environ- ment in which everyone is equal and all achievements are celebrated.

All students are expected to be punctual,attend school on all working days in proper attire. The school does not as a rule, approve of any leave to its students during the academic session except very exceptional events.

Large numbers of co-curricular activities covering all age groups are organized through the academic year to encourage participation of students.

School buses are available for students of all classes as the transport has the great influence on the school activities of pupils.

United English Boarding School believes that the best results are the mirror image and reflection of its academic competencies and brilliant that counts the most. Every year it has been consistent upward trend to produce desired 100% SEE results since 2069 BS. that defines outcome of our planned action, set to bring out the best among students of all ages alike. Now the school has been placed among the top academic institution in Makwanpur district.

OBJECTIVES

The Objective of the UEBS is to produce human resource that possesses administrative skills, problem-solving tools and the professionalism necessary to run an organization effectively whereas the primary objective of the UEBS is to prepare students to become competent, skilled, confident and disciplined.

MISSION

UEBS is driven by the mission to provide students the best knowledge, skill and practical exposure they need for success and encompass in a well rounded educational and social work environment.

CLASSROOMS

Spacious classrooms with large size windows and ultra-modern audio-visual teaching tools like multimedia projector, televisions sets , CD players etc.

MEDIUM OF COMMUNICATION

English, except for the Nepali classes, is the only medium of communication of UEBS

TEACHING METHODOLOGY

Teaching methodologies that are in practice of UEBS include discussion, explanation, illustration, communicative peer work, group work , instructive case study, project work, seminar, a\v presentation etc .

DISCIPLINE-CODES OF CONDUCT

UEBS pledges to give its students confidence in their own ability.We promote and faster self-discipline and self-responsibility. We expect all the students to be well disciplined both in and outside school. .
